About Me

Hi Nice To Meet You

Hello! I'm Thabo, and I'm absolutely thrilled to be diving headfirst into the world of front-end development. Websites have always captivated me. From their sleek designs to their ability to inform, entertain, and connect people, the web holds a certain magic. This fascination goes back to my early days, where I spent hours on captivating websites, wondering how developers managed to arrange information and content in such a visually appealing and interactive way.

That early curiosity sparked a passion for front-end development. This field allows me to combine my love for problem-solving with the ability to bring ideas to life on the web. As a fast learner with a knack for tackling challenges, I'm constantly hungry to expand my skillset. I'm particularly drawn to building dynamic user interfaces with JavaScript frameworks and creating captivating animations with CSS.
